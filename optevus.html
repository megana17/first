Our Features

OPTEVUS is a cognitive intelligence-driven talent management software that enhances the entire recruiting experience and helps the staffing services value chain by providing Job search, Algorithmic resume ranking, Video screening and interviewing, online Assessments and Proctoring, as well as augmented intelligent recommendations.

Quick Interview Process

2x increase in fill rate, 40% reduction manually pre-screening resumes, total recruitment time on one job requisition and average number of candidates interviewed for each role.

Video Interviews

Easy for candidates to apply by accessing a link and recording responses to the employer’s initial questions, with the ability for hiring managers to schedule ad-hoc interviews and assessments on demand. It enables recruiters, hiring managers and candidates to connect in live video interviews that are saved to the candidates profile and accessible to anyone the client/ hiring manager wishes to share the session.

Feedback

Feedback categories are designated by the client and feedback is requested from all interview panel members for each candidate interview that results in reduced bias, provides a weighted score, and is stored in the platform for future reference.

Assessments and Online Proctoring

External integration with test collaboration tools layer in the ability for recruiting managers to launch Technical, Coding, Behavioral, Psychometric assessments, and more, from a plethora of questions that are covered for various roles and skills. We help you stay on top of the game with our AI-powered assessments that automatically detect fraudulent behavior. Automated impersonation and multiple people detection, plagiarism reports as well code playback for identifying suspicious coding practices are just some features found within these tests!

Integrations

Optevus is integrating with multiple vendor management systems, applicant tracking systems, assessment tools and background companies to cover the complete lifecycle of the hiring process in order to create an efficient one-stop platform for our client’s hiring needs.
